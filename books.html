How to download chapter PDFs (quick guide)

NCERT serves chapter-wise and complete-book PDFs on its official pages. To get the PDFs:

  1. Open the subject page (links above).
  2. On the NCERT page, click the "Download complete book" link or each chapter's PDF link.
  3. The files are watermarked and hosted on NCERT's domain; you may save them for personal use.

I performed a best-effort extraction of direct chapter-PDF links, but NCERT serves these pages dynamically and the PDFs are not reliably exposed to static fetches. Use the subject links above to download chapter PDFs from NCERT's official pages.
